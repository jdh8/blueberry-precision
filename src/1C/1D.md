# Negative 1♦

There have been many variants to fold very strong hands into 1♣-1♦-1♥, such as
Terrorist's Moscito and Viking Precision.  All these variants successfully
rescue the 2NT opening from the notrump ladder, freeing 2NT up for preemptive
uses.

| 1♣-1♦- | ART NEG |
|--------|---------|
| 1♥!    | ART F, 20+
| 1♠     | 16--19, 5+♠ or UNBAL 4=♠
| 1NT    | 16--19, BAL
| 2♣♦♥   | 16--19, UNBAL 5+#
| 2♠!    | 16--19, 4+♥, 6+♦♣
| 2NT!   | 16--19, 5♥(440)
| 3♣♦!   | 16--19, 5+#, 5+♥
| 3♥!    | 16--19, 6+♥, 4+♠
| 3♠     | 16--19, 7+♠
