# Non-forcing 1NT

Opener may rebid a 3-card minor due to protection from the impossible 2♠.

| 1♥-1NT  | CONST--INV, 0--3♠, 0--3♥ |
|---------|--------------------------|
| 2♣      | 5+♥, 3+♣ or BAL 13--15
| 2♦      | 5+♥, UNBAL 3+♦
| 2♥      | MIN, 6+♥
| 2♠      | MAX, 4+♠, 5+♥
| 2NT     | MAX, non-SOL 6=♥
| 3♣      | MAX, 5+♣, 4+♥
| 3♦      | MAX, 5+♦, 5+♥
| 3♥      | MAX, 6+♥, S-SOL or 7+♥
| 3NT!    | MAX P/C, 6+♣, 4=♥
| 3♠, 4♣♦ | MAX, 5+#, 6+♥
